# 3. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## 1. Basic Principles

You must use the service legally, compliantly, honestly, and safely. You are responsible for all requests, consumption, outputs, and subsequent use initiated through your account, API key, access token, console, system integration, or other credentials.

You must evaluate the legality, safety, accuracy, and suitability of inputs, outputs, model choices, business processes, end-user scenarios, and usage results yourself.

You must not bypass, damage, evade, abuse, or interfere with GGUUAI, upstream model providers, cloud service providers, payment channels, network routes, content safety systems, access control systems, rate-limit systems, or risk-control systems.

## 2. Prohibited Uses

You must not use the service to engage in, assist, facilitate, generate, distribute, automate, or attempt to perform the following activities.

## 2.1 Illegal, Non-compliant, and Infringing Activities

You must not use the service to conduct, facilitate, assist, conceal, or encourage any illegal crime, regulatory violation, infringement, law-enforcement evasion, disruption of public order, or harm to the lawful rights and interests of third parties.

You must not use the service to generate, process, distribute, or assist in distributing content that infringes copyrights, trademarks, patents, trade secrets, portrait rights, reputation rights, privacy rights, data rights, or other lawful rights and interests.

You must not use the service to process, generate, or distribute data, files, code, images, audio, video, business materials, personal information, or other content that you have no right to use, submit, disclose, or process automatically.

## 2.2 Fraud, Scams, and Improper Transactions

You must not use the service for fraud, scams, phishing, impersonation, false advertising, fake orders, spam, malicious marketing, stolen-card use, chargeback fraud, money laundering, cash-out activity, payment-rule evasion, abnormal refunds, malicious arbitrage, or other improper transactions.

You must not impersonate any individual, organization, platform, customer service representative, government agency, financial institution, payment institution, merchant, or other entity to deceive, mislead, induce, or manipulate others.

You must not generate, optimize, automatically send, or distribute at scale scam scripts, phishing links, fake customer service content, fake investment content, fake recruitment content, fake prize content, fake refund content, or other deceptive content.

## 2.3 Cybersecurity Abuse

You must not use the service to develop, distribute, improve, deploy, debug, or evade detection of malware, trojans, ransomware, viruses, worms, backdoors, botnets, credential theft tools, unauthorized access tools, exploit tools, persistence-control tools, or other harmful programs.

You must not use the service to perform or assist unauthorized scanning, credential stuffing, brute-force attacks, authentication bypass, privilege escalation, data theft, system intrusion, denial-of-service attacks, supply-chain attacks, phishing attacks, social engineering attacks, or other acts that damage, interfere with, control, or compromise computer systems, networks, accounts, applications, interfaces, or data.

You must not attack, stress test, scan, scrape, replay, interfere with, bypass, abuse, or damage GGUUAI, upstream model providers, third-party cloud platforms, payment channels, network routes, other users' systems, or any third-party system.

## 2.4 Critical Infrastructure and Public Safety

You must not use the service to harm, damage, interfere with, attack, or attempt to control critical infrastructure, including but not limited to communications, energy, transportation, healthcare, water supply, finance, government, public safety, elections, education, cloud computing, data centers, or other important systems.

You must not use the service to generate or optimize operating instructions, attack plans, evasion plans, or automated workflows that may directly endanger life and health, public safety, property safety, infrastructure stability, or social order.

## 2.5 Child Safety

You must not use the service to create, obtain, distribute, index, classify, describe, trade, promote, evade detection of, or glorify any content that harms child safety, involves child sexual exploitation, child sexual abuse material, grooming of minors, child nudity, child pornography, sexualized minors, or other harm to minors.

If GGUUAI reasonably believes that related content involves child-safety risks, GGUUAI may immediately restrict, suspend, terminate, preserve necessary records, and report to competent authorities or relevant institutions.

## 2.6 Violence, Hate, Terrorism, and Extremism

You must not use the service to incite, assist, promote, glorify, organize, or carry out violence, terrorism, extremism, serious harm, self-harm, suicide, hate, harassment, bullying, or discrimination against protected groups.

You must not generate threats, intimidation, doxxing, harassment plans, violent mobilization, extremist propaganda, terrorist recruitment, weaponized hate content, or dehumanizing expression targeting specific individuals or groups.

## 2.7 Privacy, Identity, and Surveillance Abuse

You must not use the service to illegally collect, process, infer, identify, track, monitor, disclose, trade, or abuse personal information, sensitive personal information, non-public information, identity credentials, financial information, communication records, location information, biometric information, or other protected data.

You must not use the service for unauthorized facial recognition, identity recognition, private-area recognition, profiling after data leaks, tracking, harassment, identity theft, social engineering attacks, or other acts that infringe privacy and identity rights.

You must not use the service for illegal surveillance, mass surveillance, political censorship, illegal law-enforcement assistance, illegal discriminatory screening, or other uses that violate laws, regulations, and fundamental rights.

## 2.8 Misinformation, Impersonation, and Public Impact

You must not use the service to generate, optimize, distribute, or automatically distribute at scale false, inaccurate, misleading, forged, or deceptive content that may harm economic, political, social, public health, public safety, or public-interest outcomes.

You must not use the service to impersonate real individuals, media, experts, institutions, governments, public-interest organizations, or other trusted sources in order to create false credibility.

You must not use the service for public opinion manipulation, fake interactions, fake traffic, paid manipulation groups, coordinated inauthentic behavior, targeted vote suppression, disruption of public participation, misleading public decisions, or large-scale influence over political, judicial, economic, or social outcomes.

## 2.9 Weapons, Biological, Chemical, Nuclear, and High-risk Technologies

You must not use the service to design, develop, manufacture, obtain, improve, test, deploy, or evade control of regulated weapons, explosives, military-grade cyber capabilities, biological weapons, chemical weapons, nuclear weapons, radiological hazards, missiles, weaponized drone systems, autonomous weapon systems, or other technologies that may cause personal injury, public safety risks, or national security risks.

You must not use the service to generate operating steps, formulas, processes, parameters, evasion methods, procurement plans, or automated execution plans that directly improve the high-risk capabilities above.

## 2.10 Sexually Explicit and Illegal Pornographic Content

You must not use the service to generate, distribute, trade, optimize, or automatically distribute illegal pornography, sexual exploitation, non-consensual sexual content, deepfake pornography, sexualized minors, sexual violence, secretly recorded content, coercion, harassment, or other content that violates laws and regulations, platform rules, or third-party rights.

Legitimate sexual health, reproductive health, scientific, educational, medical, artistic, or public-interest scenarios must not be used to evade the restrictions above.

## 2.11 Platform Abuse and Resource Evasion

You must not, without authorization, batch-register accounts, share accounts, transfer accounts, resell accounts, resell API keys, resell credits, provide proxy top-ups or proxy payments, hide the real user, hide the real purpose, falsify end users, rotate IPs, split requests, bypass rate limits, evade billing, evade risk control, abuse discounts, maliciously consume resources, or build unauthorized competing services.

You must not attempt to reverse engineer, copy, scrape, replay, stress test, extract, bypass, damage, or interfere with GGUUAI's systems, documentation, interfaces, model routing, billing systems, content safety systems, risk-control systems, or upstream service restrictions.

## 3. High-risk Scenario Requirements

If you use the service in legal, medical, mental health, financial, insurance, employment, housing, education, examination, certification, news publishing, public safety, public service, law-enforcement-related, identity recognition, credit, loan, benefit eligibility, important resource allocation, or other scenarios that may have a significant impact on personal rights, life and health, property interests, opportunity fairness, or public interest, you must take additional safety measures.

Outputs involving advice, judgment, recommendation, scoring, screening, ranking, diagnosis, treatment, insurance underwriting, claims, credit, loans, hiring, admission, qualification determination, news publishing, or other matters that may affect personal rights and interests must be reviewed by personnel with appropriate qualifications, experience, or professional capability before dissemination, execution, or final determination.

If model outputs are displayed directly to end users, consumers, customers, patients, students, job applicants, applicants, or the public, you should clearly disclose in an appropriate place that the content is AI-generated or AI-assisted. For interactive chatbots, external AI assistants, or consumer-facing AI agents, you should disclose their AI nature at least at the beginning of each session.

You must not use model outputs as the sole factual basis, legal basis, medical basis, investment basis, credit basis, hiring basis, admission basis, law-enforcement basis, news fact basis, or final decision basis without independent verification.

You should set necessary access controls, content review, log auditing, rate limits, human fallback, appeal mechanisms, risk notices, abnormal interception, data protection, and compliance review processes based on your business scenario.

## 4. Use Related to Minors

If your product, service, website, application, or system allows minors to directly or indirectly use capabilities that include the GGUUAI service, you must comply with applicable minor protection, privacy protection, content safety, parental consent, age-rating, and local legal requirements.

You must not use the service to induce, manipulate, exploit, sexualize, bully, harass, track, identify, profile, or harm minors.

## 5. Agents, Tool Calls, and Automated Execution

If you use the service for AI agents, automated workflows, tool calls, plugins, MCP services, browser operations, code execution, file operations, payment operations, account operations, message sending, or other scenarios that can automatically execute external actions, you must ensure that you:

1. Set clear permission boundaries;
2. Require human confirmation for high-risk actions;
3. Apply authentication and authorization controls for external system access;
4. Prevent models from exceeding permissions, misoperating, repeatedly executing actions, or being manipulated by prompt injection attacks;
5. Record necessary logs for auditing, troubleshooting, and loss control;
6. Do not use agents or tool calls to bypass prohibited uses under this Policy.

## 6. Violation Handling

If GGUUAI reasonably believes that you or your end users have violated this Policy, made abnormal calls, attacked, committed fraud, abused the service, created payment risks, regulatory risks, upstream restriction issues, data risks, content safety risks, or other security risks, GGUUAI may take one or more of the following measures depending on the circumstances:

1. Restrict, downgrade, delay, reject, interrupt, or block related requests;
2. Suspend, disable, rotate, delete, or restrict API keys, access tokens, or other credentials;
3. Restrict, freeze, suspend, or terminate accounts, models, interfaces, orders, top-ups, refunds, or balance handling;
4. Require you to supplement identity information, organization information, order information, payment proof, business purpose, compliance explanation, or other necessary materials;
5. Cooperate with upstream model providers, payment channels, cloud service providers, regulators, judicial authorities, or other legally authorized authorities by providing necessary information;
6. Immediately terminate the service for serious, urgent, or uncorrectable risks.

If you believe a related measure was mistaken, you may submit an appeal through [support@gguuai.com](mailto:support@gguuai.com). The appeal should include account information, order or request ID, specific issue, business-purpose explanation, relevant proof materials, and contact information. GGUUAI will conduct a reasonable review within 1 to 7 business days after receiving complete materials. Complex matters related to security, payment, upstream providers, or regulation may take longer.
