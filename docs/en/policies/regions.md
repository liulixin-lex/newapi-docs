# 6. Service Availability and Regional Policy

## 1. Basic Position

GGUUAI is committed to serving all regions where service is permitted by law, supported by payment channels, allowed by upstream service policies, supported by cloud platforms and network environments, and approved by platform risk assessment.

Service availability may be affected by applicable laws and regulations, regulatory requirements, sanctions and export-control rules, payment channels, upstream model provider policies, cloud platform regional restrictions, network environments, identity review, industry requirements, and platform risk-control requirements.

Even if a region can access GGUUAI pages, the console, documentation, or some services, this does not mean that all models, interfaces, payment methods, top-up methods, refund methods, channels, context lengths, tool capabilities, image or file capabilities, cache capabilities, fine-tuning capabilities, or other service capabilities are available in that region.

## 2. User Responsibilities

You must confirm yourself whether your location, your organization's location, your end users' locations, the location of your payment method, your actual business operating location, and your specific use scenario are allowed to access and use GGUUAI services.

You must not use the service in any country, region, use case, industry, entity, or target where use is prohibited by applicable laws and regulations, regulatory rules, sanctions restrictions, export controls, upstream model provider policies, or platform rules.

You must not bypass country, regional, payment, compliance, sanctions, export-control, or risk-control restrictions through VPNs, proxies, jump hosts, false identities, falsified addresses, proxy payments, proxy top-ups, account transfers, shared keys, hidden real entities, hidden real purposes, or other methods.

If your business serves multiple countries or regions, you must ensure that you and your end users comply with local laws and regulations, industry norms, platform rules, and upstream model provider policies.

If you are unsure whether a region, industry, entity, or use case is available, you should consult GGUUAI through the contact email before use.

## 3. Payment Channels and Top-up Availability

Payment channels, top-up methods, refund methods, arrival times, handling fees, exchange rates, bank processing, payment institution review, and risk-control rules may differ depending on country, region, payment institution, bank, receiving method, payer, or policy changes.

Some regions may be unable to use certain payment methods, top-up channels, refund channels, or receiving methods.

Payment channels may reject, delay, freeze, return, reverse, or review transactions according to their own rules. GGUUAI is not responsible for payment channel rules, bank processing, exchange-rate changes, cross-border payment restrictions, or third-party fees.

If payment or refund requires supplementary orders, identity information, payment information, receiving information, purpose, or other proof, you must provide true, accurate, and complete information as required by GGUUAI.

## 4. Export Controls, Sanctions, and Prohibited Targets

You must not directly or indirectly provide, export, re-export, transfer, sell, or use GGUUAI services for any country, region, entity, individual, industry, or use prohibited by applicable laws and regulations, sanctions rules, export-control rules, or regulators.

You must not allow sanctioned, restricted, prohibited, or materially compliance-risky individuals, entities, organizations, government agencies, military entities, or other targets to directly or indirectly access the service.

If GGUUAI reasonably believes that continuing to provide the service to you may violate applicable sanctions, export controls, regulatory requirements, payment channel rules, or upstream policies, GGUUAI may immediately restrict, suspend, or terminate the service.
